IT Ethics

In the field of Information Technology, ethics plays an important role in ensuring responsible use of technology. Developers must protect user data, respect privacy, and write secure code.

Data scientists must avoid bias in algorithms, ensure fairness, and use data responsibly. Ethical decision-making helps prevent harm and builds trust in technology systems.
